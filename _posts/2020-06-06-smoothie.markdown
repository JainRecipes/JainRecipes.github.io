Make an awesome smoothie and enjoy!

#### Serves 2 people

#### Ingredients

|  Ingredients |   Quantity   |
|:------------:|:------------:|
|    Bananas   |       2      |
| Strawberries |       6      |
|     Dates    |       3      |
|     Sugar    |     None     |
|    Yogurt    |    1 spoon   |
|     Milk     |   0.75 cup   |
|  Chia seeds  | 0.5 teaspoon |

## Directions

1. Put everything in a vitamix blender.
2. Mix everything for a minute or so.
3. Serve and enjoy.
